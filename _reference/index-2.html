How it works

Three steps to hiring clarity

Upload. Simulate. Understand. HIRVO closes the gap between how you think you look and how recruiters actually see you.

01

Upload your resume

Paste or upload your resume. HIRVO parses it exactly as an ATS and human recruiter would — no assumptions, no sugar-coating.

02

Receive a pass / borderline / fail verdict with specific, actionable rejection reasons and a structured fix plan for each issue found.
