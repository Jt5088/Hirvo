How it works

Find your rejection reason in under 5 minutes

No coaching fluff. No generic tips. HIRVO runs the real filters recruiters use on your resume, then shows you what failed and how to fix it.

01

Upload your resume

Paste or upload. HIRVO parses it the same way Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever do — then shows you what a recruiter actually receives versus what gets silently dropped.

02

Pick your recruiter type

Startup hiring manager. Enterprise HR screener. University recruiter. Each one screens differently, flags different things, and rejects for different reasons. Pick the one you keep losing to.

03

Read the rejection report

Pass, borderline, or fail — with a specific reason for every verdict. Not “add more keywords.” The recruiter’s actual thought, the exact bullet that failed, and what to rewrite to flip it.
