Methodology

Built on how recruiters actually screen — not how they say they do

HIRVO's recruiter models are built from real screening behavior data — not LinkedIn advice threads or career coaching intuition. Every feedback rule maps to a pattern we've documented in how candidates actually get filtered.

Zero optimism bias
HIRVO doesn't soften the verdict. You get the internal monologue of a recruiter who's seen 200 resumes this week and is already behind schedule.
Role-specific logic
A startup hiring manager doesn't care about GPA. An enterprise screener might reject you for a 4-month gap. Each profile runs different rules on your resume.
Silent filters, made visible
Visa risk flags, school tier bias, career trajectory doubts — HIRVO names the things recruiters act on but never put in a rejection email.
